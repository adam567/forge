Briefing To-Do Reading List Business Plan

FORGE — To-Do List

Order of operations. Do these in order. Don't skip ahead.

Phase 0: Free Things First

This Week Total cost: $0

Read the DIU Counter-Drone Sensing CSO
Go to diu.mil/work-with-us. Find the Feb 2026 counter-drone sensing solicitation. Read the requirements. Understand the format. FREE
Read the Army C5ISR Acoustic Detection RFI
They want passive acoustic detection for dismounted troops. This is a description of our product. Find it on SAM.gov. FREE
Download and run a drone audio detection repo
acoustic_uav_threat_detection_CNN — clone it, install dependencies, run inference on sample audio. See what the ML pipeline looks like. Runs on any laptop. FREE
Download the DroneAudioset
23.5 hours of drone audio, MIT license. Listen to what drones sound like to a microphone at various distances. FREE
Create a SAM.gov account (login.gov)
Just the account, not full registration yet. Get your login set up. Takes 10 minutes. FREE
Read Freedom's Forge chapters on Kaiser's shipyard approach
You already have the book. Focus on how he broke the problem into producible modules using existing commercial components. That's our approach. FREE
Browse open xTech competitions
Go to xtechsearch.army.mil. See what's open. xTechCounter Strike is the target. Submissions are short white papers. FREE

NOT YET — Don't Do These Early

Hold

Don't quit the day job
You need income. The compliance skills are the differentiator. Quit when revenue replaces salary, not before.
Don't pursue security clearances
Not needed for detection-only work. Pursue only when a specific contract demands it.
Don't pursue MIL-STD certification
Prototype contracts accept commercial-grade. Invest $10-50K in testing when a production contract demands it.
Don't take loans or seek VC
$10K gets you to prototype. xTech prizes and DIU/OTA contracts are the funding path. Non-dilutive first.
Don't hire anyone
Stay lean. Contract out PCB design if needed. Don't staff up before revenue.
