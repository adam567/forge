Generate a podcast that explains why counter-drone detection is an urgent and growing market opportunity in the United States. Cover three themes in order: First, how cheap drones transformed warfare in Ukraine and what that means for US defense. Second, the specific and immediate drone threat at the US-Mexico border from cartels. Third, why acoustic detection — listening for drones by sound — is emerging as a critical capability because the newest drones (fiber-optic and autonomous) are invisible to the radio-frequency detection systems that dominate the market today. End with what the US government is actively spending money on right now — specific programs, grants, and contract vehicles — and why there is a gap in the market for a low-cost networked acoustic sensor that no Western company currently sells.
